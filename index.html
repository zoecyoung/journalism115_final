about zoe young

a discussion about who I am

the beginning

I was born into a family of two artists. Because I was surrounded by creative atmospheres during my first years, I had always known I wanted to be an artist. My inspirations come from the people around me. I believe that "everything happens for a reason" and "your experiences shape you to who you are." Because I surround myself with other creative individuals, I continuously want to create something.

childhood drawings

My first sketchbook is from 2003. I would be seen constantly drawing weird figures with long toes and fingers and belly buttons. My drawing hasn't changed much when I think about it. I am zoning in on specific details about people when drawing them.

my work now

I can still be seen with a sketchbook or an ipad in my hand. I constantly get inspired by the people around me, so I constantly want to be drawing. My work now consists of bright colors, musical lyrics, and cartoons.
